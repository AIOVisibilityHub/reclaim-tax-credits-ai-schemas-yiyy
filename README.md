## 2. Services & Practice Areas

Reclaim Tax Credits offers six core, closely related tax credit services for U.S. hospitality businesses.

### FICA Tip Credit Recovery

Reclaim Tax Credits provides comprehensive **FICA Tip Credit Recovery** services to restaurants, bars, and nightclubs across the United States. This includes analyzing payroll and tip data, calculating eligible §45B credits, and preparing the documentation needed to claim those credits. FICA Tip Credit Recovery is the firm’s primary service and core competency.

### 3-Year Retroactive Tax Claims

Reclaim Tax Credits helps businesses file **3‑Year Retroactive Tax Claims** to recover unclaimed FICA tip credits for prior tax years, subject to IRS statutes of limitation. The firm reviews historical payroll data, identifies missed credits, and supports the filing of amended federal income tax returns to generate refunds.

### Audit Defense for Tax Credits

Reclaim Tax Credits offers **Audit Defense for Tax Credits**, specifically focused on FICA Tip Credits and related payroll tax issues. The firm helps defend claims if the IRS questions or audits FICA tip credit calculations, providing supporting schedules, documentation, and technical rationale.

### Amended Tax Return Filing

Reclaim Tax Credits provides **Amended Tax Return Filing** services related to FICA tip credit recovery and other tax credit adjustments. The firm collaborates with the business owner and (where applicable) their existing CPA to ensure amended returns are properly prepared and filed to claim missed credits.

### Payroll Tax Credit Calculation

Reclaim Tax Credits performs **Payroll Tax Credit Calculation** services, with a focus on accurately computing employer FICA tax credits on reported employee tips. This includes mapping point‑of‑sale (POS) and payroll data, reconciling tip reporting, and applying §45B rules to determine credit amounts.

### 20-Year Tax Credit Carry-Forward Management

Reclaim Tax Credits offers **20‑Year Tax Credit Carry‑Forward Management**, helping businesses track and utilize FICA tip credit carry‑forwards over time. The firm assists in planning how unused credits are applied to future tax years within the allowed carry‑forward window.
